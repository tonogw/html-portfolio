Secure Host-to-Host CBEST Terminal Integration (KSEI-Citi)

SWIFT Cost Optimization through Process Correction (Deutsche Bank)

Background: SWIFT billing showed unusually high charges, prompting investigation into underlying causes.

Problem Statement: Incorrect SWIFT environment routing caused unnecessary billing related to test and production message handling.

Approach: Conducted deep-dive analysis, identified the incorrect ON/OFF switching mechanism controlling SWIFT test environments, and designed a corrected operational process.

Impact: Prevented an annual unnecessary cost of USD 150,000 and restored proper environment handling.

Skills Used: SWIFT expertise, analytical investigation, process correction, operational risk mitigation.

Legacy 3270 Automation for Remittance & Operations

Background: Operational staff used IBM 3270 terminal for routine tasks, many of which were repetitive and time-consuming.

Problem Statement: High volume of manual keystrokes introduced fatigue, errors, and inefficiency.

Approach: Built automation macros using IBM HLLAPI scripting combined with Excel VBA triggers to streamline multi-step routines.

Impact: Significantly reduced manual keystrokes, lowered error rates, and increased throughput without requiring system changes.

Skills Used: VBA automation, HLLAPI scripting, workflow optimization.

IoT Utility Recording Architecture Design (3 Towers Residences)

Background: Utility meter reading was performed manually by engineering staff, requiring over 5 working days each month.

Problem Statement: Manual processes limited billing timeliness, accuracy, and real-time visibility.

Approach: Designed a scalable IoT architecture utilizing Modbus RS485, Node-RED monitoring, and connected smart meters. Proposed workflow changes for end-to-end process improvement.

Impact: Proposed system could reduce process time from 5 days to under 1 hour, enabling billing issuance on the first day of each month. Improved data accuracy and operational efficiency.

Skills Used: IoT system design, business process re-engineering, technical architecture documentation.
